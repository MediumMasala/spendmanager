# Privacy Policy - SpendManager

**Last Updated**: [Date]

## Introduction

SpendManager ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Phone Number**: Used for account authentication via OTP
- **WhatsApp Number**: Optional, for receiving weekly summaries

### Information Collected Automatically

#### Notification Data
When you enable notification access, we capture transaction notifications from financial apps you select. By default:
- **Redacted text is stored**: Account numbers, card numbers, and reference IDs are masked
- **Raw text is NOT stored** unless you explicitly enable this option

#### Device Information
- Device type and OS version
- App version
- Timezone and locale

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Contacts
- Location
- Photos or media
- SMS messages (in Play Store version)
- Call logs

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Functionality
- Parse transactions from notifications
- Categorize spending
- Generate weekly summaries
- Deliver WhatsApp summaries (if opted in)

### We Do NOT
- Sell your data
- Share data with third parties for advertising
- Use data for profiling beyond app functionality

## Data Processing Modes

### Local-Only Mode (Default)
All data stays on your device. No information is uploaded to our servers.

### Cloud AI Mode
When enabled:
- Redacted notification text is uploaded
- AI processes transactions for better categorization
- Data is stored encrypted on our servers

### Upload Raw Mode (Off by Default)
If explicitly enabled:
- Unredacted notification text may be uploaded
- Provides better parsing accuracy
- Can be disabled at any time

## Data Storage and Security

### On-Device
- Data stored in encrypted local database
- No data backup to cloud (Android backup disabled)

### On Servers
- Data encrypted at rest
- Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
- Access restricted to authorized personnel

## Data Retention

- **Event data**: 90 days
- **Transaction summaries**: 1 year
- **Account data**: Until account deletion

## Your Rights

### Access
You can export all your data from the app settings.

### Deletion
You can delete all your data at any time:
- In-app: Settings > Delete All Data
- Contact: privacy@spendmanager.app

### Portability
Export your data in JSON format.

### Consent Withdrawal
Disable cloud features at any time in Settings.

## Third-Party Services

### OpenAI
- Used for transaction parsing (Cloud AI mode only)
- Redacted text only
- No data stored by OpenAI (store: false)

### WhatsApp Business Platform
- Used for sending weekly summaries (if opted in)
- Only sends pre-approved template messages

## Children's Privacy

SpendManager is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
